Want an Otherworldly Experience?

Just as astronauts did in 1969, you can also take an out–of–this–world expedition to a whole new place. At The Extraterrestrial Cabins, we value the calm and quiet that outer space can offer, which is rarely found in your day–to–day life.

You have the option to take your journey in one of our quality flying saucer cabins, which come in two different styles. Nestled on the edge of an enchanting forest, you can be transported to a planet of peace and tranquility. The allure of the surrounding nature will make you feel as if you are in another world.

Explore the Possibilities

Whether you’re an adventurer or a lounging aficionado, The Extraterrestrial Cabins has activities for all:

  • Delve into the inner world of the nearby forest by hiking the trails.
  • Take a canoe for a calming ride down the river.
  • Light up the fire pit and gather around for s'mores and hot chocolate.
  • Set your sights on Venus, Mars, or Saturn through a telescope (perhaps you’ll even see E.T. going home.)
  • Venture out once the sun sets to see if you can add your own story to the UFO sightings in the area.



Your Journey Through Nature

Step into a portal to the captivating world of Mother Nature’s beauty. Our hiking trails are suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced travelers, with trails ranging from 3 miles to a lengthy 30 miles. Marked maps, signs, and mile markers direct your journey, but if you prefer a guided hike, we have master trail leaders who will happily lead your hike. Remember to keep an eye out for local wildlife—you might see various animals, including, but not limited to:

  • Mammoth Moose
  • Etheral Elk
  • Brave Bald Eagles
  • Radient Red foxes
  • Rapid Rabbits
