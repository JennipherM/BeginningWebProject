Canoeing

Floating over the calm surface of a tranquil lake you will be surrounded by vibrant views of lush forests and wildlife. Whether you’re seeking adventure with friends, an undisturbed retreat alone, or a special day out with family, canoeing provides either a thrilling exploration or a peaceful experience.


With the sun glinting off the water and the sounds of nature all around, every paddle stroke becomes an invitation to connect with the world. Plan a picnic on a secluded shore or challenge your companions to a friendly race; each outing can be uniquely tailored to your mood.


Museum

Stepping through the entrance of the alien museum, you are met with artifacts from distant galaxies. Each exhibit lures you in, showcasing intricate technology that defies understanding, while strange skeletal life forms loom over you, casting eerie shadows in the dim lighting. Interactive screens invite you to delve deeper into the history and function of each artifact, making every visit a unique experience. With a gift shop brimming with themed merchandise, including books, toys, and memorabilia, it's the perfect place to take home a piece of the cosmos.

The museum will be open Monday through Friday from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. and Saturday and Sunday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., ensuring ample opportunity for enthusiasts of all ages to engage with the wonders of the universe.





April Festival

Join the locals on April 26th—April 30th every year to celebrate National Alien Day! A vibrant alien-themed parade transforms the streets into a luminous display of intergalactic wonder. Colorful floats decorated with shining stars and oversized alien figures travel through the crowds. Street vendors serve up cosmic dishes, featuring "galactic green" treats and drinks that sparkle like a starry night.

Families can enjoy interactive activities like alien costume contests and a scavenger hunt for hidden extraterrestrial treasures. Don’t miss out on the chance to explore alien-themed art installations displayed throughout the neighborhood, showcasing the creativity and imagination of local artists inspired by the mysteries of the universe.
